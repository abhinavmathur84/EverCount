Privacy Policy

EverCount — Last updated March 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information EverCount collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Information We Collect

EverCount does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data you enter — event names, dates, notes, and tags — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Advertising

EverCount displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to serve personalised ads. You can limit ad tracking at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking on your iPhone.

For more information on how Google uses data, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, EverCount schedules local notifications on your device. These are processed entirely on-device and no notification content is sent to any server.

Widgets

The Home Screen widget reads event data from a shared App Group container stored locally on your device. No data leaves your device.

Analytics

EverCount does not use any third-party analytics SDKs. We do not track how you use the app.

Children's Privacy

EverCount is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at abhinav@abhinavmathur.net.
